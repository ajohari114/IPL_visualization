Are you a cricket stats fnatic? Do you find yourself over IPL debates among your friends? How much time do you spend convincing your friend that Royal Challengers Bangalore ain't winning anything? (sorry) If you're a passionate cricket enthusiast, then this website is perfect for you!
These interactive visualizations provide valuable insights into long-time rivalries, team's consistency, and how much the location of a match can play a role in deciding the outcome of the game!

Use this interactive visualization to explore the Head-to-Head between teams! The links between the teams are colored proportionally to show the win-rate ratio at a glance! For e.g. Sunrisers Hyderabad has never won against Lucknow Supergiants (unbelievable right?), so the entire line is black. If you need specific stats, hover over the links to get the exact stats!

Ever wondered who the most consistent teams are, or how frustratingly close Lucknow and Punjab make games (sorry KL Rahul fans)? This visualization shows the margin teams usually win games by.

It was terrible to bat first in Mumbai back when the IPL started in 2008, and now? It's a batsman's paradise! Check out how the win-rates of states has changed when teams bat first in the IPL! A greener state means venues and stadiums on average have a high batting-first win-rate (and red is bad...)
